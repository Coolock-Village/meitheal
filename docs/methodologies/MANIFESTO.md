# Meitheal Manifesto

Meitheal is a cooperative task and life engine for the home.

It is intentionally Astro-first, local-first, and Home Assistant-native. It exists to reduce friction in real life operations, not to replicate corporate management software.

## Mandatory Cultural Rules

- Use DDD ubiquitous language in UI, code, and docs.
- Ship KCS updates with every behavior change.
- Prefer composable OSS integrations over bespoke reinvention.
- Optimize for durability on modest hardware.

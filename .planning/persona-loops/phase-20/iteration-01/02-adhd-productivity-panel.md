# ADHD/Productivity Panel - Phase 20 Iteration 01

## Objective

Reduce contributor cognitive load while preserving strict architecture standards.

| Persona | Recommendation | Impact (1-5) | Effort (1-5) | Risk (1-5) | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Workflow Coach | Add visual indicator to Kanban view showing count of sub-tasks for Epics/Stories | 5 | 3 | 1 | Accept |
| Execution Coach | Add keyboard shortcuts (E, S, T) while in task detail panel to rapidly switch task types | 4 | 2 | 2 | Accept |
| Knowledge Coach | Add inline documentation about the Agile hierarchy in the Kanban settings or empty states | 3 | 2 | 1 | Defer |
| Focus Optimizer | Visually dim unrelated tasks when hovering over an Epic or Story card | 3 | 4 | 2 | Defer |
| Automation Coach | Automatically set `parent_id` & `task_type` when creating a new task using the "+" button inside a swimlane | 5 | 3 | 2 | Accept |

## Capture/Triage/Follow-through Improvement

- Contextual task creation: Auto-assigning parent/type based on the active swimlane completely removes the manual step of linking child tasks during triage.

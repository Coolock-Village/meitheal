# ADHD/Productivity Panel — Phase 17 Iteration 01

## Objective
Make a large audit cycle executable without overload while preserving rigor.

| Persona | Recommendation | Impact | Effort | Risk | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Execution Coach | Timebox each domain audit pass to 45-minute blocks with explicit done criteria. | 4 | 1 | 2 | Accept |
| Focus Optimizer | Use a single findings register format to reduce switching across docs/tools. | 4 | 1 | 2 | Accept |
| Cognitive Load Analyst | Batch similar checks (API, UI, docs) rather than hopping by file. | 3 | 1 | 2 | Accept |
| Friction Identifier | Pre-generate command bundles for baseline verification runs. | 3 | 1 | 2 | Accept |
| Knowledge Coach | Require each finding to include reproduction steps and verification owner. | 4 | 2 | 3 | Accept |
